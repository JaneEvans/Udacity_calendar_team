Growing Together

This site was put together in early 2018 by a group of students from the Seattle area taking the "challenge course" section of Udacity's Front End Web Development Nanodegree program, as part of the Grow with Google scholarship.


We are ecstatic to continue our learning journies in tech.


Building this site inspired us to push our capacities as developers and effective team members to the next level. We hope it might help you find and share opportunities for inspiration and for learning, for growth, and for community, too.
